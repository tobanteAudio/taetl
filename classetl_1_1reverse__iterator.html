Detailed Description

template<class Iter>
class etl::reverse_iterator< Iter >

etl::reverse_iterator is an iterator adaptor that reverses the direction of a given iterator. In other words, when provided with a bidirectional iterator, etl::reverse_iterator produces a new iterator that moves from the end to the beginning of the sequence defined by the underlying bidirectional iterator.

This is the iterator returned by member functions rbegin() and rend() of the standard library containers.
