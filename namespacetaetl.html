◆ move()

template<class T >
constexpr auto taetl::move ( T &&  t) -> typename taetl::remove_reference<T>::type&&
noexcept

taetl::move is used to indicate that an object t may be "moved from", i.e. allowing the efficient transfer of resources from t to another object. In particular, std::move produces an xvalue expression that identifies its argument t. It is exactly equivalent to a static_cast to an rvalue reference type.
